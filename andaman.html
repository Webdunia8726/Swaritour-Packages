7 Days - Andaman Tour

  • Day 1 : Arrival At Port Blair - Corbyn's Cove - Port Blair

    Once you reach Andaman via Port Blair airport, you will be transferred to the hotel. After a brief rest, you will be taken to Corbyn’ Cove beach. This is the only beach in Port Blair. Enjoy your time in Corbyn’s Cove at leisure.

    Later, you will be taken to the light and sound show of Cellular Jail to watch a presentation about the independence struggle of India and heroic sacrifices of the freedom fighters.

    Back to hotel. Overnight stay at Port Blair.

  • Day 2 : Havelock Sightseeing (RadhaNagar Beach)

    By early morning, you will be taken to Havelock Island from Port Blair via ferry. It takes 1.5-2.5 hours to reach Havelock Island from Port Blair. Once you reach Havelock Island, you will be taken to a pre-booked hotel.

    After a brief rest at the hotel, you will be taken on an excursion to Radhanagar Beach or Beach number 7. This beach is very famous for its aesthetic beauty and is named as Asia’s best beach by the Time Magazine.
    After enjoying beach activities, you will be taken back to your hotel. Overnight stay at Haveloc

  • Day 3 : Havelock (Elephant Beach)

    The day is at leisure. You can choose to enjoy your day at any of the beaches or take rest in your hotel.

    Optional tour: By early morning, you will be taken to Elephant Beach for snorkeling. You will be driven from your hotel by 7 in the morning to the jetty, where you will take a mesmerizing boat trip for 20 minutes before you reach the snorkeling spot. The boat stops at a good shallow water region lush with coral life. This is an excellent spot for beginners in snorkeling too.

    After snorkeling, enjoy a leisure time at the beach. Later, you will be taken back to the hotel in Havelock.

    Overnight stay at Havelock.

  • Day 4 :Havelock-(Neil Island)

    After breakfast, the check-out formalities start and you will reach the jetty to board a ferry to Neil Island. Once you reach Neil Island, you will be checked into a hotel.

    After a brief rest, you will be taken on a sightseeing trip to Bharaptur Beach, Laxmanpur Beach, and other destinations. By late evening, you will be taken back to your hotel.

    Overnight stay at Neil Island.

  • Day 5 : Neil Island - Port Blair

    After breakfast, you will be checked out of the hotel and taken to jetty to board a ferry to Port Blair. Once you reach Port Blair, you will be taken to a hotel.

    Rest of the day is at leisure. You can choose to rest in your hotel room or explore Port Blair on your own.

    Overnight stay at Port Blair.

  • Day 6 :Port Blair Sightseeing

    After breakfast, you will be taken on a city sightseeing tour to Port Blair. The tour includes cellular jail (a pre-independence jail used by British), Chatham Saw Mill (largest and oldest saw mill of Asia), Anthropological museum, Marine museum, forest museum, Naval Marine Museum, and others.

    After lunch, you will be taken to Corbyn’s Cove beach for relaxing and enjoying beach activities. Later, you will be taken to the markets of Port Blair for souvenir shopping.

    By evening, you will be taken back to the hotel. Overnight stay at Port Blair.

  • Day 7 :Port Blair (Sightseeing)

    This day marks the end of the itinerary. After breakfast, the check-out formalities start. Later you will be dropped at airport to board the flight for onward destination.
