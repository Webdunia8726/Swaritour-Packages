Golden Triangle Tour 5 Days

  • Day 1 : Delhi

    Welcome By Our chauffeur of Golden Triangle Tour 5 Days will pick up you from Airport And Transferred To Check In The Hotel. Delhi Is A Unique Blend Of Historical Monuments And Contemporary Malls, Overnight Stay In Delhi Hotel.

  • Day 2 : Delhi - Agra

    In The Morning After Early Breakfast, Take A Thrilling Tour Of Delhi. Visit The Iconic Red Fort And Chandni Chowk, Colossal Mosque Jama Masjid, Built By Shah Jahan. Proceed To Visit Qutub Minar And Ancient Humayun's Tomb, Which Are Famous UNESCO World Heritage Sites. You, Will, Be Driven In Air-Conditioned Car, Later Visit Raj Ghat, India Gate, Parliament House, President House, Depart For Agra Via Yamuna Expressway To Cover The Distance Of Almost 210 Kms In Nearly Four Hours. Upon Arrival, Check-In The Hotel. Enjoy Dinner. Overnight Stay In Agra.

  • Day 3 : Agra - Jaipur

    Leave Early Morning By Car At 6:00 AM To Visit The Exquisite Taj Mahal During Sunrise. Its Majestic Aura And Architectural Magnificence zap you, Will. Witness The Fairy Like Beauty Of Taj Mahal, An Architectural Wonder With Beautiful Inlay Work And Elaborate Marble Screens. It Is A Historical Mausoleum Of Mumtaz Mahal Made In White Marble With Beautiful Carvings And Jalis. Come Back To The Hotel As The Sumptuous Breakfast Awaits. Check Out. Proceed To See The Massive Agra Fort Built By Emperor Akbar In Red Sandstone And Mughal Rulers Dwelled Over Here. Leave For Fatehpur Sikri, The Abandoned Capital City Of Emperor Akbar. People Soon Left This Place Owing To The Dearth Of Water. Post Mouthwatering Lunch In A Posh Restaurant, Depart For Jaipur. On Arriving, Check In The Hotel In Jaipur, Commonly Referred To As The Pink City. Enjoy Authentic Rajasthani Dinner. Overnight Stay In Jaipur.

  • Day 4 : Jaipur

    Early At 8 AM, Get Ready For A Thrilling Tour Of Jaipur. This Beautiful City Is Home To Majestic Forts, Wonderful Palaces, And Gardens. Embark For A Memorable Visit To Amer Fort, Also Known As Amer Palace. Enjoy A Thrilling Ride On The Elephant, Which Is Decorated With Colorful Saddle, To Reach The Fort Atop The Hillock Overlooking Maota Lake. The Sprawling Fort Constructed In Red Sand-Stone And Marble. See Temple Of Goddess Sila Devi At Ganesh Gate. Explore Diwan-e-Aam, Diwan-e-Khas, And Sheesh Mahal. Visit The Popular City Palace, Which Is Known For An Extraordinary Blend Of Mughal And Rajput Architecture, Hawa Mahal, Where The Rajput Women Enjoyed The Market Place From The Jharokhas. From Here, Move On To Jal Mahal, Which Is Located In The Middle Of The Man Sagar Lake And Was Built In The Year 1799 By Sawai Pratap Singh. Jantar Mantar Is Another Popular Landmark In Jaipur.

    Come Back To The Main City. Explore The Colorful Streets And Bazaars Of Jaipur To Buy Traditional Ghagra Cholis, Odhinis, Bedcovers, Exquisite Kundan Jewellery, Paintings, And Beautiful Miniatures. The Liveliness Of the Streets Of Pink City Infuse Element Of Fun And Excitement. Overnight Stay In The Hotel In Jaipur.

  • Day 5 : Jaipur - Delhi

    After Breakfast As Per Your Flight Schedule Depart From Jaipur To Reach The Airport. It Takes Almost 5 Hours To Cover Nearly 280 Kms, Depending On The Traffic. Take A Flight For Onward Destination. Take Back Lovely Memories Of These Enchanting Tourist Destinations On Your Golden Triangle Tour 5 Days.
