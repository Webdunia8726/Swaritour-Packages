Madhya Pradesh 2 Night/ 3 Day Tour

  • Day 1 : Indore

    Your Ujjain Omkareshwar tour package commences with your arrival in Indore. On arriving in Indore, you will be visiting Omkareshwar first where you will rejuvenate yourself with the healing power of its pervading spirituality. Then, proceed towards Maheshwar and visit the Maheshwar fort. After these visits, check-in to the hotel and enjoy an overnight stay.

  • Day 2 : Ujjain

    After breakfast, check out from the hotel and proceed for a drive to Mandu. Attractions included in your itinerary are Jahaz Mahal, Hindola Mahal, Jahangir Mahal, Jami Masjid, Hoshang Shah’s Tomb, Rupmati Pavilion, and Baz Bahadur Palace. After the visits, proceed for a drive to Ujjain. Upon arrival, check-in to the hotel and enjoy an overnight stay.

  • Day 3 : Indore

    After breakfast, proceed towards Ujjain local sightseeing. You will be visiting the Mahakaleshwar Temple and then you will visit the Kalbhairav temple, Harsiddhi temple, Mangalnath temple, Ram-ghat, Gopal Mandir, Ved shala. Post the visits, get transferred to Indore for your departure.
