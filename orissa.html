Odisha Tour 3 Nights 4 Days Itinerary

  • Day 1 : Bhubaneswar to Puri to Raghurajpur

    On Arrival at the Bhubaneswar airport, you’ll be met by one of our representatives outside the exit gate. The representative will take you to your car, from where you’ll be transferred to Puri and check in to the hotel. Drive to Raghurajpur visit on Sakhigopal temple & Siruli Hanuman Temple. After Lunch, Visit Local Sightseeing like Gundicha Temple, Lokanath Temple, Sonar Gouranga Temple, and Bedi Hanuman Temple. Evening visit of Lord Jagannath Temple (Non-Hindus are not allowed). Overnight at Puri.

  • Day 2 : Puri to Chilika (Satapada) to Puri

    Morning Darshan at Jagarnath Temple at around 6 AM. Drive for Chilika Lake at Satapada, the largest brackish water lake. Visit the Irrawaddy Dolphins, Sea Mouth, and Red Crab Island by taking a boat cruise. Then visit Alarnath Temple and back to Puri. Overnight at Puri.

  • Day 3 : Puri to Konark to Pipli to Bhubaneswar

    The morning after breakfast drive to Konark visit the Sun temple. THen visit Ramchandi temple and Chandrabhaga beach. Then Proceed to Bhubaneswar en-route visit to Pipili (the applique work village), Dhauli (Buddhist stupa/peace pagoda), 64 Yogini Temple & Rajarani Temple & Over Night at Bhubaneswar.

  • Day 4 : Bhubaneshwar Sightseeing - Departure

    The morning after breakfast visit famous city temples along with Lingaraj, Parsurameswar, Mukteswar temple, one of the most easily seen temples of Bhubaneswar. Then Proceed to Khandagiri & Udayagiri (the oldest rock-cut caves, the caves are reminiscent of the influence of Buddhism and Jainism in Odisha). Departure for Bhubaneswar airport.
