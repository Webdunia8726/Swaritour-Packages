UTTARAKHAND PACKAGE 6 NIGHTS 7 DAYS

  • Day 1 : ELHI-NAINITAL (7 HOURS DRIVE)

    Start the tour from Delhi to Nainital. After making it to Nainital, which is a charming hill station in Uttarakhand, check in to a hotel. Freshen up and go out to enjoy boating in Naini Lake. Located at an elevation of 6358 ft. above sea level, it is a paradise for both young and old alike.

  • Day 2 : NAINITAL

    After having delicious breakfast, we will take you for a sightseeing, which includes the most important places such as Naina devi Temple, Naini Lake. And don’t forget to buy exquisite items from the famous shops of the Mall road.

  • Day 3 : NAINITAL- CORBETT (2 HOURS DRIVE)

    A morning drive to Corbett National Park where you can enjoy wildlife safari, which takes you deep in the woods and offers you a glimpse of a variety of animal species such as barking deer, chital, Indian grey mongoose, langur, Indian pangolins and Himalayan black bears. Jim Corbett is the perfect place for both nature lovers and wildlife enthusiasts. Evening will be spent at the hotel where you can take pleasure in an array of recreational facilities.

  • Day 4 : CORBETT

    Wakeup early morning for a jeep safari to explore the wild animals in the jungle. In the afternoon visit Corbett Museum and Garjiya Devi Temple. Further, you will also enjoy bird watching tour at Corbett. Overnight stay in the hotel.

  • Day 5 : CORBETT – MUSSOORIE (6 HRS DRIVE)

    After breakfast proceed for Mussoorie. On arrival check into a hotel situated amid the picturesque surroundings. Overnight stay at the hotel.

  • Day 6 :MUSSOORIE

    After having delicious breakfast, head off to the famous attractions of Mussoorie like Mall Road, Gun Hill Point and Kempty Falls. In evening back to hotel for a comfortable overnight stay.

  • Day 7 : MUSSOORIE- DELHI

    Post breakfast check out from hotel and drive back to Delhi after breakfast. We will leave you to the airport or railway station for your trip back home.
